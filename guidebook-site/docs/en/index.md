<section class="ud-section" markdown="0">
  <h2 class="ud-section__title">Why This Guidebook</h2>
  <p class="ud-section__lead">A winning proposal is not writing that brags about <strong>"how good the proposer is."</strong> It is writing that <strong class="ud-hl">"understands exactly what the funder needs, speaks in the funder's language, and answers with evidence."</strong> This book collects the thinking frames and checklists for building that "exact understanding" and "evidence-based answer."</p>
</section>
